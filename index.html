How skillEX works

Post what you need done, browse tasks from others, and complete them to earn credits. Use those credits to get help with your own projects—no cash required.

  • Browse local swaps around campus and across the University of Michigan campus.
  • Earn credits by helping with everyday tasks.
  • Spend credits on things you need — from tutoring to home repairs.
  • Strengthen your sense of community through mutual aid and shared experiences.
